Predict Revenue/Rating of movies

Data Wrangling

We first clean our data. The inputs we used are listed as follows:

  • budget
  • popularity
  • year
  • day of published year
  • duration
  • language
  • genre
  • country
  • company
  • director
  • writer
  • actor

    • Feature Selection

    We then did a feature selction.

    The most frequent variables are not necessarily the most important features for the predictors. The number of important features is reduced to about 300 after this selection process.


    Accuracy of different predictors

    We used GridSearchCV to tune our models.

    For XGBOOST model, the R2 score of revenue predictor can achieve 77% and the R2 score of rate predictor can achieve 61%.
